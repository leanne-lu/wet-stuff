cleaning up
the murky
waters of
Manchester

a collective of Mancunians working together to freshen up our water-ways, in all-ways.

2025 GOALS:

a water collection and recycling system to make use of all of the Mancunian rain

pure water fountains on the city streets to allow people to fill up their water bottles

a money return incentive for recycling plastic bottles, off the streets and out of general waste

a clean up of our canals, rivers and lakes to make Manchester's bodies of water swimmable

help us make this happen by volunteering with us!
